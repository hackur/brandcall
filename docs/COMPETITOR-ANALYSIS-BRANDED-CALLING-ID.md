### Pricing Model

BCID itself doesn't publish pricing. Their model:

> "Participating Onboarding Agents will publish fees for branded display, and Enterprises pay for calls when the Terminating Service provider confirms that their data was delivered."

**Key Insight**: Pricing is:
- **Partner-dependent** â€” Each Authorized Partner sets their own fees
- **Opaque** â€” No public pricing page
- **Delivery-based** â€” Pay only for confirmed deliveries

### How They Package Features

No tiered packaging visible. The ecosystem approach means:
- All features available through any Authorized Partner
- No "starter" vs "enterprise" visible distinction
- Everything goes through the same ecosystem

### Contract Terms

Not visible on website. Likely:
- Contract through individual Authorized Partners
- Enterprise-style agreements
- Custom terms per partner

### What We Can Learn

1. **"Pay for confirmed delivery"** is a compelling billing model â€” consider offering it
2. **Opaque pricing creates friction** â€” our transparent pricing is an advantage
